Rapid Rush Rafting

Our mission is to provide unforgettable whitewater experiences, combining extreme safety with the adrenaline of the world's most challenging rapids.

History

Founded in 1994 by a group of river guides in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, Whitewater Warriors began with a single inflatable raft and a passion for the rapids. Over the past three decades, we have expanded our fleet and expertise, guiding over 50,000 adventurers through the most exhilarating river runs on the planet.
