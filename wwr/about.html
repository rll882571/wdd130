Rapid Rush Rafting

Our mission is to provide unforgettable whitewater experiences, combining extreme safety with the adrenaline of the world's most challenging rapids.

History

Founded in 1994 by a group of river guides in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, Rapid Rush Rafting began with a single inflatable raft and a passion for the rapids. Over the past three decades.

Over the years, the company grew from a small local operation into a trusted rafting experience for visitors from around the world. Today, Rapid Rush Rafting continues to provide safe, exciting, and unforgettable adventures on the river.
