Ceará - Brazil

Ceará is a vibrant state in northeastern Brazil, famous for its stunning coastline and iconic dunes. Its capital, Fortaleza, serves as a major cultural and economic hub. The region is widely recognized for its rich traditions in craftsmanship, unique local cuisine, and its growing importance in education and technology.
